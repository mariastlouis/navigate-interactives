How it works:

Data

The foundation of a great data map is the data that it's based on. You know your data and the story behind it better than anyone and we’re happy to work with the data you have to make it usable for a map form. If you’re having trouble getting the data you want, we can point you in the right direction or help you file an open records request to get it.

Analysis

We love this part because it’s where the real stories come out. Tell us the questions you want answered and the story you hope to tell with the data. We can help analyze it if you need and we’ll also clean it, so it’s consistent, and format it, so it will be ready for mapping.

Choose

Now that we have the data ready to go, it's time for you to choose the type of map you want, the technology you want to use and the typography and color scheme to match your brand. If you're not sure, don't worry, we'll give recommendations to ensure that you have the best type of map for your data.

Map

This is where the data stories come alive. We take the data we have and plug it into the map format you chose. We do all of the coding so you don’t have to trouble yourself with it.

Review

Take a look at your map, suggest style changes and or other revisions. You get the final word before you map goes public.

Share

Take your completed map and share it with the world. We’ll give you the code to embed the map on your site and also advice on how to best use social media to your advantage to share the map with others.
