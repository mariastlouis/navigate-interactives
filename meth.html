PORTFOLIO >> METH HOUSES MAP

MARKER MAP: Meth houses in the Pikes Peak region

About this map

To create this map, we first converted a PDF into a spreadsheet and then cleaned the data and re-structured it to make it mappable.

Findings: Law enforcement have reported 331 known houses in the Pikes Peak region where methamphetamine was distributed. The majority of the discovered homes are in areas that historically have lower income levels.

Options for similar maps: Even if your data is in a PDF, there's a good chance we can crack it and make it interactive. Marker maps are a great option if you have individual addresses for data.
