About us

The idea for Navigate Interactives came from both passion and frustration: a love for interactive data maps and an understanding of the challenge it can be to make them. We come from a background of journalism and have a love affair with open data. We are happiest when we get new numbers we havenâ€™t seen before, find the trends in them and then plug the numbers into maps to make the trends easier to see. Unfortunately, the technology required to make a really great map is often a challenge. Our job is to simplify the process of making these maps as much as possible so our customers can keep their focus where it needs to be: on their message.

Our core beliefs:

Data can and should tell a story

Data and code should be open

Public information should be accessible
